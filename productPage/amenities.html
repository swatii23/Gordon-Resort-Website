Our Amenities

The (McCarthy) Beach

The beach is delightful to sit in overlooking the River-Ethope. Cool down in the crystal clear pool, enjoy marvels of Abraka.
Gordon Resort Hotel, Abraka, Delta State, Nigeria, provides you with “McCarthy Beach” – the place of choice for your vacations, picnics, weekend getaways, games, parties, outdoor social and corporate events, etc.
Swim or be sailed in our live boat around the safe waters of River Ethiope. Feel the difference natural and artificial waters.
Enjoy a very chilled bottle of bear or soft drink at our beach bar as you are caressed by the soft-cool wind of a natural river.
Visit our Barbeque and Hot Sausage Beach Spot, Phosky's Cocktails and get taste of the good life. The aroma, and taste will blow your mind and the taste shall last forever. Do you need to heat-up before diving into the beach? No worries, we have several beach games such as volleyball, football, paintball, and so much more.

Our Restaurant

We serve delicious food from the local cuisine made with natural ingredients from local farms, including vegetarian-friendly items!
Enjoy the best breakfast in town served daily from 06h30. Lunch and dinner are served until 23:00.
Come and enjoy your favourite beverage or a mug of coffee at the coffee bar.
Fancy a bite in your room? Order a meal and drinks from room service – available all day.

Our Conference Room

Our conference room is fully equipped to make your meetings as comfortable as possible.
Better yet, all corporate meetings and events at Gordon Resort Hotel are managed and organized by a dedicated team who are delighted to work with you on all aspects of creating a memorable and distinguished event. Every detail from arrival to departure is considered, and our packages are fully customizable to fit your needs and your vision. So, whether you are accommodating a group of 5 to 50, we welcome you to tour our beautiful facilities, and look forward to creating an event to remember.

Night Club and Exclusive Lounge

Retreat to our night club, fet into your groovy shoes or sink into our comfy chairs with a glass of your favorite tipple from 7.00pm.
Rooted in nature, our nightclub and exclusive lounge curates an experience for the senses. Experience the beauty that surrounds you through natural elements that make up each hand-crafted and carefully selected piece. Experience the subtle and perfect beauty of nature through every sip, every laugh and every bite.

Our Catering Services

We offer our guests hotel and catering options for their special events, wedding and business functions. We offer catering services for both at the resort or outside events. contact us us for our catering menu for more information on what we offer, including sample menus and other options.

Gordon Resort Hotel popularly known as McCarthy Beach is located in the University town of Abraka in Delta State. Set out in large expanse of land of over 65 hectares the resort boasts of over 90 exquisitely appointed and tastefully furnished Rooms/Suites.
